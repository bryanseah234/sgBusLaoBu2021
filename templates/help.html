  1. What is Bus LaoBu?

    It is a web application which helps you find the nearest bus stop,
    that has bus service(s) to get you to a MRT station.

  2. Why LaoBu?

    As they say, all is not lost until mum can't find it.
    Bus LaoBu will act as your mum, to find you a bus service, to get you to an MRT station.

  3. Why is my location data needed? And what happens if I don't share?

    LaoBu will need access to your GPS in order to make accurate calculations.
    If you do not allow LaoBu to use your location data,
    you can also key in coordinates for LaoBu to work with.

  4. What if I have more questions?

    No worries! LaoBu is here to answer any of your queries.
    Input your name, email and what you would like to tell LaoBu in the form below.
    LaoBu wil take 3 working days to response.
